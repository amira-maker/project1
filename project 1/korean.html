Noodle Dishes
Japchae (잡채)
Sweet potato starch noodles, spinach, carrots, mushrooms, soy sauce, sesame oil, beef, onions
Japchae was once considered a royal dish in Korea.
170 EGP
Jjamppong (짬뽕)
Spicy seafood broth, noodles, squid, mussels, shrimp, vegetables, chili oil
Jjamppong’s fiery color comes from Korean chili flakes!
190 EGP
Jajangmyeon (자장면)
Noodles, black bean paste, pork, onions, zucchini, cucumber
Jajangmyeon is famously enjoyed on “Black Day” in Korea.
165 EGP
Kalguksu (칼국수)
Hand-cut wheat noodles, broth, vegetables, chicken or seafood
Kalguksu means “knife-cut noodles” in Korean.
175 EGP
Naengmyeon (냉면)
Chewy buckwheat noodles served cold with tangy broth, cucumber, egg, beef slices
Naengmyeon is especially popular in hot Korean summers.
185 EGP
Ramyeon (라면)
Instant noodles in a spicy broth with egg, scallion, vegetables
Ramyeon is Korea’s favorite comfort food and snack.
