Soups & Stews (국과 찌개)
Kimchi Jjigae (김치찌개)
Kimchi, pork, tofu, scallions, garlic, chili powder, broth
Kimchi Jjigae is often made with leftover, well-fermented kimchi.
180 EGP
Soondubu Jjigae (순두부찌개)
Soft tofu, seafood, egg, chili flakes, garlic, green onions, broth
Soondubu Jjigae is loved for its silky tofu texture and spicy kick.
185 EGP
Doenjang Jjigae (된장찌개)
Soybean paste stew with tofu, vegetables, mushrooms
A daily staple in Korean homes.
155 EGP
Galbitang (갈비탕)
Beef short rib soup, glass noodles, radish, scallions
Galbitang is prized for its rich, clear broth.
200 EGP
Samgyetang (삼계탕)
Whole young chicken stuffed with rice, ginseng, jujubes, boiled in savory broth
Samgyetang is believed to boost energy in Summer.
225 EGP
Budae Jjigae (부대찌개)
"Army base stew" with sausage, ham, kimchi, instant noodles, beans
A fusion stew popular since the Korean War.
160 EGP
Extra Sauces (추가 소스)
Kimchi (김치)
Fermented napa cabbage, Korean chili flakes, garlic, ginger, scallions, fish sauce.
Kimchi is eaten at almost every meal in Korea!
10 EGP
Gochujang Sauce (고추장)
Fermented Korean red chili paste, glutinous rice, soybeans, and salt.
Gochujang gives Korean food its signature spicy kick.
15 EGP
Seaweed Strips (김)
Roasted and lightly salted sheets of seaweed (laver).
Seaweed is often used as a wrap for making gimbap.
20 EGP
Tteok (떡)
Chewy cylinders made from glutinous rice flour; often eaten in spicy sauce.
Tteokbokki is one of Korea’s most popular street foods!
25 EGP
Fish Cake Sticks (어묵)
Ground fish, flour, vegetables, and seasoning formed into sticks and boiled.
Fish cakes (eomuk) are commonly served on skewers at Korean markets.
30 EGP
Ssamjang (쌈장)
Spicy, savory paste made from soybean paste, chili, garlic
Commonly used for grilled meat lettuce wraps.
18 EGP
Soy Sauce Dip (간장)
Salty soy sauce with vinegar, spicy pepper slices
A must-have for dumplings and pancakes.
12 EGP
Side Dishes (반찬)
Japchae Salad (잡채 샐러드)
Glass noodles, fresh vegetables, soy-sesame dressing
Japchae makes a tasty cold salad alongside main plates.
75 EGP
Korean Potato Salad (감자샐러드)
Mashed potatoes with carrots, cucumber, egg, mayonnaise
A staple Korean banchan, mild and creamy.
60 EGP
Egg Roll (계란말이)
Rolled omelette with bits of veggies
Popular in lunchboxes and picnics.
70 EGP
Korean Pickled Radish (단무지)
Crunchy sweet-and-sour yellow pickles
Common as a palate cleanser in BBQ meals.
35 EGP
Korean Pancake (전)
Savory pancake with green onion, seafood, or vegetables
Jeon is a beloved party and snack food.
60 EGP
